About Hello Texas Records

Hello Texas Records is an independent Texas music label built on one simple idea: great music doesn't need a zip code. Our roster spans country, rock, blues, pop, EDM, Latin, jazz, Christian worship, and beyond.

We sign artists we believe in and give them the freedom to make the music they need to make. Every genre. Every story. One label.
