About

Sawyer Price

Sawyer Price makes country music the way country music used to get made — unhurried, honest, built around stories that actually happened to somebody.

His sound lives somewhere between Red Dirt Texas and front porch folk: acoustic at the root, full band when it earns it, lyrics that don't try too hard because they don't need to. He writes the kind of songs that sound like they've always existed.

Signed to Hello Texas Records, his debut album Unrushed is exactly what the title says — music made on its own time, in its own way, for people who've got somewhere to be but know the drive is the point.
